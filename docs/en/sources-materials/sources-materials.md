# 32. Sources and Materials

> **Version:** `0.3.1`  
> **Status:** in development

Sources are grouped into one continuously numbered list. Preprints and industry publications are not treated as conclusive evidence; they are used as empirical inputs, threat models, and directions for pilot validation.

New sources are added to the end of the list. Source numbers are cited in chapter text independently of chapter order and are not renumbered without separate approval.

The content of external materials, especially GitHub repositories, may change after the access date. A link may therefore no longer lead to the exact revision used in developing the methodology. The access date records the point in time on which the author relied. When reproducibility is particularly important, also record a commit, tag, release, or archived copy of the material.
